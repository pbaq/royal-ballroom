What is Royal Ballroom?

Royal Ballroom is a ballroom dancing school for people aged over 75. We aim to transform the lives of our students by helping them be more active, form lasting bonds, and have a lot of fun!

What is Ballroom Dancing?

Ballroom dancing is done in couples. It includes waltz, tango, rumba, quickstep, and many others.

Dancer of the Week

Dahlia McLoughlin started dancing with us five months ago. Her kids convinced her and her husband William to try ballroom dancing after they retired. Now they are an active part of our classes and are preparing for the annual Ballroom Competition this coming September. Good luck to you both!

In Dahlia's words:

"I got tired of seeing my husband lie around doing nothing after he retired. Now we both dance and we love it! It has made us closer to each other, our grandkids love seeing us dance, and it's great exercise!"
