Group Classes

The size of group classes ranges from 10 to 16 students. These are not ideal for students with very specific needs, as the larger class does not allow for personalized attention.
We have 3 different levels of classes:

  • Beginner: These are for students with no experience in dancing or less than a year of experience. We start with the basics and go up from there.
  • Intermediate: These are for students with one or two years of experience. We work on increasing technical ability and start on more complex aspects.
  • Expert: These are for students with more than three years of experience. We primarily work on learning new dances and honing the skills students already have.

Individual Classes

Individual classes are provided for students of all dance levels. You can sign up to have a lesson on your own or with a partner.
These classes are more personalized to the needs of the students because of the one-on-one attention.
